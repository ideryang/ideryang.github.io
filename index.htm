To create work that communicates. To make things simpler, smarter, more helpful, more beautiful and more fun. To think before we act. To work with people, not for people. To be part of change.

MECE (Mutually Exclusive, Collectively Exhaustive) principle is one of the main way to help me think and understand.

I believe that to truly solve a problem you need to understand it, pull it apart, then put it back together again. You need to look at it from every angle and try to delivery possible solution. You need to discuss and debate it. And I believe in doing all of these things together.

Consideration, conversation and collaboration.

That’s my process.
