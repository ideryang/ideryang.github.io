Color Contrast Checker

The Color Contrast Checker is another front-end project by myself.

This tool is built for designers and developers to test color contrast compliance with the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.0 as set forth by the World Wide Web Consortium (W3C). All calculations are based on the formulas specified by the W3C.

To run your test, select a foreground color and a background color. The results will automatically generate and you will receive your scores. You will receive a pass, margin or fail, depending on your selections.
