Redesign AliExpress
for Checkout Process

Overview

Checkout process has always been one of the most complicated process in AliExpress. For last three years, with constant collobration with Google Payment Team, PayPal and Yandex Wallet, the checkout process became heavier and even more complicated. At the same time, more localized services such as local pickup points and flash delivery appeared with the booming up of new marketplaces. Thus, it’s a good time to make some changes from the designer’s perspective and pish it forward.

The Challenge

The checkout process is one of the most complicated process in AliExpress: with hundreds of pages for the address selection and payment options. The engineers and PD hold the attitude for years: no change if there is no deadly issues. Thus, a big & bold design solution should be provided based largely on the bussiness itself.

My Approach

My process started from the page audit. Which can help me as well as PD and dev get to know the current situation of checkout process.

Page Audit

From different dimensions, I conduct an UX page Audit to see the current situation of checkout process.





Competitor Analyze

Competitor Analysis is a good way for us to learn from other apps and websites. Attached are apps that within the range of my competitor Analysis.

The Result
